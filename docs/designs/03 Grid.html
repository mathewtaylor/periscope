Ten of them fit on a 1440 monitor without collapsing — the 10-session worst-case is comfortable, not compressed. State is communicated by a top edge-color on the tile (cyan / lavender / amber) and a single breathing status dot; the sparkline shows recent shape so you can see a session's rhythm at a glance. Session detail drops into a familiar lane timeline + a right rail of summary numbers. The closest reference is small multiples from a statistics textbook — many units, one grammar.
